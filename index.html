Lufia II Rise of the Sinstrals

Storyline

The game is a prequel to Lufia & the Fortress of Doom. It follows the story of the first main character's ancestor, Maxim, and explains the origins of the war between mankind and a group of gods called the Sinistrals.

Stats

Magic Points. Once it reaches reaches, cannot cast a spell

DFP

AGL

INT
